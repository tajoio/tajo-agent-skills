# Campaign Review Checklist

## Audience

- Who receives the message?
- Which list, segment, or event qualifies them?
- Which suppression and unsubscribe filters apply?
- Is there a test audience before production?

## Consent

- What is the consent basis?
- Is consent channel-specific?
- Does the message include required unsubscribe or preference language?
- Are regional requirements understood for the audience?

## Content

- Is the subject line accurate?
- Is the message useful to the recipient?
- Are personalization variables valid?
- Are links trackable and safe?
- Are claims supportable?

## Delivery

- Is the sender verified?
- Is the expected volume within rate limits?
- Is there a staged rollout plan for large sends?
- Are webhook events mapped back to the dispatch?

## Post-Send

- Track delivered, bounced, complained, unsubscribed, opened, and clicked events.
- Update suppression state from permanent negative events.
- Summarize failures with enough detail for replay or remediation.
